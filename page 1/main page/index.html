Facilities

Physics Lab

Our physics lab offers advanced instruments and practical setups, enabling students to explore mechanics, electricity, and optics through hands-on experiments.

Chemistry Lab

Our school’s chemistry lab is fully equipped with modern tools, safety equipment, and ample workspace. Students access high-quality apparatus, reagents, and resources.

Biology Lab

Our biology lab provides microscopes, specimen samples, and essential equipment, allowing students to study cells, anatomy, and ecology in a practical environment.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is equipped with modern computers, high-speed internet, and essential software, enabling students to develop skills in coding, research, and digital literacy.

Bus Facilities

Our school provides safe and reliable bus facilities with well-maintained vehicles, trained drivers, and planned routes to ensure convenient transport for students.

Digital Classroom

Our school offers digital classrooms with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and internet access, enhancing learning through multimedia resources and engaging educational content.

Canteen

Our school canteen provides a variety of healthy and hygienic food options, ensuring balanced meals and snacks for students and staff..

Drinking Water

Our school ensures access to clean, filtered drinking water through multiple dispensers, promoting health and hydration for students and staff.

CCTV

Our school is equipped with CCTV cameras throughout the campus, ensuring safety and security for students, staff, and school property.
